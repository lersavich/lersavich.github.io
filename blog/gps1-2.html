What is Pair Programming?
Pair programming is the concept of working with another programmer with established roles. There are significant benefits to pair programming, such as: solidifying knowledge or gaining knowledge from your partner, increased scrutiny of code, sanity checking of concepts.

Why is Pair Programming Important at DBC?
Pair programming is important DBC because it introduces a concept that is common in Ruby development. It also has the benefit of reinforcing learned concepts through the process of walking someone else through them.

Comparison of Driver and Navigator
In paired programming the driver will be the person doing to coding. The navigator is there to help guide the driver through the process of development as well as to make suggestions when necessary.
